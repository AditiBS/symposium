Solution f4: Forestation

Planting Trees for a Greener Future

A blend of reforestation (replanting trees) and afforestation (planting trees in new areas). Expanding global forest cover sequesters carbon, enhances biodiversity, and restores ecosystems. Implement community-led tree-planting drives, incentivize sustainable agroforestry, and protect existing forests through policy. Simple, scalable, and nature-based, this solution tackles CO2 emissions while supporting livelihoods.
