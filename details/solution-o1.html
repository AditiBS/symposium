Solution O1:Ocean Afforestation

Carbon Sequestration Through Seaweed Farming

Ocean afforestation proposes cultivating large-scale seaweed farms to absorb atmospheric CO2, with the intent of sequestering that carbon by sinking the biomass into the deep ocean. However, significant scientific uncertainties and potential ecological risks remain regarding its feasibility and long-term impacts.
