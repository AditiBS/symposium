Solution J2: Afforestation

Planting Trees for a Greener Future

"J2 GreenGrid" is a decentralized renewable energy network that integrates solar, wind, and battery storage into microgrids, empowering communities to generate and trade clean energy via blockchain. By optimizing local energy use and reducing reliance on fossil fuels, it cuts emissions while boosting resilience. **Key Benefits:** - **Decarbonization** – Replaces coal/gas dependence. - **Energy Democracy** – Lets communities profit from surplus power. - **Scalability** – Adaptable for urban/rural areas. **Implementation:** Pilot in high-sun/wind regions, incentivize adoption via tax breaks, and use AI for demand-supply balancing.
