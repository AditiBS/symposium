Solution W1: Widespread Wetland Restoration

Restore and protect wetlands

Restore and protect wetlands globally to sequester carbon, enhance biodiversity, and buffer against floods. Wetlands store more CO₂ than forests and reduce extreme weather impacts. Governments and communities should prioritize conservation, rewild degraded areas, and enforce anti-drainage policies. .
