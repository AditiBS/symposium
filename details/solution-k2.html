Solution K2:Kelp Forest Cultivation for Carbon Sequestration

Planting Trees for a Greener Future

Cultivating kelp forests in coastal areas to absorb CO₂ through rapid photosynthesis. Kelp grows quickly, storing carbon in biomass, which can then be sunk to the ocean floor or used for biofuel, reducing atmospheric carbon. This scalable, natural solution also supports marine biodiversity and reduces ocean acidification.
