C4: Carbon-Conscious Commuting

Shift to Zero Emission Transport

Promote cycling and electric public transit by implementing city-wide "C-Bike & E-Bus" networks. Dedicate lanes for bikes and electric buses, subsidize e-bike purchases, and integrate renewable-powered charging stations. Shifts urban transport from fossil fuels to zero-emission options, reducing traffic congestion and carbon footprints while improving air quality.
