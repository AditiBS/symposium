Solution W3:Widespread Adoption of Water-Efficient Agriculture

Grow More with Less – Cutting Emissions, Saving Every Drop

*"Widespread Adoption of Water-Efficient Agriculture" Solution:Transition to water-smart farming techniques like drip irrigation, rainwater harvesting, and drought-resistant crops to reduce water waste and methane emissions from flooded rice fields. Implementation:Governments and NGOs should subsidize water-efficient technologies, train farmers, and enforce sustainable water policies. Logic:Agriculture consumes 70% of global freshwater and emits methane. Efficient water use cuts emissions, conserves resources, and boosts resilience to droughts—mitigating climate change while ensuring food security.
