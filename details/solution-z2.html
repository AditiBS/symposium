Solution Z2: Zero-Zone Urban Planning

Transforming Urban Areas

Implement "Zero-Zone Urban Planning" (Z2UP), a strategy designating car-free zones in cities (marked as Z2 districts) powered by 100% renewable energy, dense green spaces, and walkable/bikeable infrastructure. Z2UP reduces emissions, cuts urban heat, and promotes sustainable living. Pilot in high-traffic areas, then expand. Summary: Z2UP transforms urban cores into zero-emission hubs, eliminating fossil fuel reliance in targeted zones while improving livability. Scalable and measurable, it combines zoning laws, clean energy, and transit redesign for rapid climate action.
