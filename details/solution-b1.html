Solution B1: Biochar

Biochar for Carbon Sequestration

Boost the use of **biochar**—a charcoal-like substance made from organic waste—to capture and store carbon in soil. Biochar improves soil fertility while locking away CO₂ for centuries. Farmers can produce it through pyrolysis (heating biomass without oxygen) and integrate it into agriculture, reducing emissions and enhancing crop yields. Scalable and low-cost, this method tackles climate change while supporting sustainable farming.
