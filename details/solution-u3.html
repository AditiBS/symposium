Solution U3: UNIVERSAL GREEN ROOF MANDATES

Planting trees ON TOP OF BUILDINGS for a Greener Future

Mandate green roofs on all new/renovated urban buildings to absorb CO₂, reduce energy consumption via natural insulation, and mitigate heat islands. By integrating vegetation atop structures, cities can manage stormwater runoff and lower ambient temperatures. Governments can enforce this through zoning laws or tax incentives, accelerating adoption. This scalable approach combats climate impacts while cutting emissions, conserving energy, and enhancing urban resilience—transforming rooftops into eco-friendly assets that support biodiversity and sustainable city growth.
