J5 Green Jetstream Initiative

Planting Trees for a Greener Future

Implement AI-powered "J5" high-altitude drones to seed cirrus clouds with eco-friendly particles, thinning them to allow more heat escape into space. These solar-powered drones operate in the jetstream, targeting key atmospheric regions to enhance Earth’s albedo and cooling effects. Scalable, low-cost, and reversible, this geoengineering tactic buys time for carbon drawdown.
