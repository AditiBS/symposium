Solution U5: Urban Green Corridors

Planting Trees for a Greener Future

Urban green corridors are integrated networks of vegetated spaces within urban environments, designed to enhance biodiversity, improve air quality, and promote sustainable urban development. 2 Urban green corridors are interconnected networks of green spaces, such as parks, riversides, and greenways, within cities. They provide ecological, social, and health benefits, promoting biodiversity, improving air quality, and offering recreational spaces for urban residents. They enhance urban sustainability and livability.
