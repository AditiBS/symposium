Solution A1: Afforestation

Empowering communities. Reducing atmospheric CO2

Establish community-driven Carbon Capture Cooperatives (CCC) where local groups fund and manage small-scale carbon capture projects. These cooperatives would use affordable technologies like biochar production, afforestation, or direct air capture units. Members contribute labor, resources, or investments, earning carbon credits in return. CCCs decentralize climate action, empower communities, and create green jobs while directly reducing atmospheric CO₂. Scalable and adaptable, this model turns carbon removal into a collective effort.2
